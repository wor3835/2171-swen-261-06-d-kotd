To build and start webCheckers:
	type "mvn compile exec:java" into command line

To access the application and connect to server:
	goto localhost:4567 in any browser search bar

To play against someone:
	open an incognito/private browsing window and
	goto localhost:4567

Additional instructions:
	to play a game you must go to the signin page
	and enter a name
	Only alphanumerics allowed (no spaces or _)
	Then you will be redirected to home where you 
	can challenge any other signed in player
